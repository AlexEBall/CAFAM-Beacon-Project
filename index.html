An Old-Fashioned Craft-Cocktail Workshop with Bar Mattachine

Sunday March 4th, 2 to 5pm

DTLA's Bar Mattachine leads a special mixology workshop teaching three variations of crafting an Old-Fashioned cocktail. Participants will create their own drinks, taste test each cocktail, and get their own toolkit to make drinks at home. Take-home toolkit includes strainer, shakers, bitters, and cocktail-mixing spoons.
$80 / $70 members (includes ingredients, tools & spirits). Advanced registration required.
